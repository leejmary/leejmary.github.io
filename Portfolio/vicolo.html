Overview

Vicolo Tours is an Italian tours company, serving business-to-business needs, that curates customized, guided tours of the hits and secrets of Italy. In this assignment, a small team of visual and UI designers were each tasked with creating two new landing marketing page designs, as well as a destination page of the chosen design, in less than two weeks. As a new front-end developer, I took on the challenge of developing the completed and approved design of my two options to display to the client how I envisioned website interactivity.

Process

To begin, we asked the client for marketing landing page inspiration, or companies that the client found had demonstrated their services in a fluid and easily ingestible way. In the interim, we each created our own mood boards to inspire us in our own designs. (Image 1. My pin board of inspirational elements shown right/above.)

The client provided us a work-in-progress PSD that included necessary sections, images, and copy. These materials were helpful as we were able to better understand the content Vicolo Tours was trying to communicate to its users. With this information, I created my own wireframes (Image 2. One of many sketches/low-fidelity wireframes, which ended up being utilized in the final piece. First image shown below.) that more fluidly communicated the service and mission of Vicolo Tours. I also created a style tile (Image 3. Second image shown below.) to help convey how I envisioned the final deliverable.
