Overview

Great Lakes Brewing Company is a fictional brewery located in Chicago, IL. For this assignment, I took a bottle of whiskey with a pre-existing logo of Great Lakes, crafted by our UI Design Instructor, to create a digital environment for the brand.

Process

I was never a big consumer of alcohol (give me coffee all day every day!). As a result, the first two days of the week-long project were spent researching major beer labels as well as local breweries. I leveraged my findings to design a one-page marketing site that spoke to a more on-trend, sophisticated audience while maintaining the home-feel of a local brewery by using milder colors. (Image 1. Great Lakes Brewing Co. style guide shown right/above.)

Once a general layout had been determined for both desktop and mobile devices (Image 1. Initial sketches shown right/above.), I explored the various API endpoints made available through Spotify and decided to include the following: (1) the originally searched artist's most popular track; (2) the ability to follow the originally searched artist; (3) the originally searched artist's top four related artists and (4) their respective most popular tracks; and (5) the ability to play each of these tracks directly from the website application (provided the user is logged into his/her own Spotify account).

Image 2. The first half of the Great Lakes page on mobile-view.

Image 3. The second half of the Great Lakes page on mobile-view.

Design

The final piece is a completely responsive web application that includes all aforementioned features built on the Materialize CSS framework.
